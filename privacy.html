Flyway — Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

Flyway keeps your information on your device. We have no servers that collect it, we use no analytics or advertising, and we do not sell or share your data.

What Flyway accesses, and why

  • Location — used on your device to estimate how much migratory-bird traffic passes your window and to center the map on your area. Your location is not sent to us or stored on any server we control.
  • Camera & photo library — used only if you choose to attach a photo to a collision report. Photos stay on your device and are not uploaded.
  • Reports, saved windows, and building sides you create are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Third-party services

  • Maps. The app displays maps using tiles from CARTO/OpenStreetMap. Loading a map sends the map area being viewed to that provider, governed by their privacy policies.
  • External links. If you choose to file a report with dBird, or open the American Bird Conservancy or IWRC resources, you leave Flyway and those third-party websites handle any information you provide, under their own policies. Flyway does not submit reports on your behalf.

What we do not do

No accounts or sign-in. No analytics or tracking. No advertising. No data sales. No personal data collected by us.

Children

Flyway is a general-audience utility and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Changes

We will update this page and revise the effective date if our practices change.
